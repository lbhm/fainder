**Explanation of the Format:**

Each predicate is a 3-tuple: (comparison operator, percentage, reference number). For example:

* **(>=, 0.1, 10):** At least 10% of the values are greater than or equal to 10.
* **(>, 0.95, 200):** More than 95% of the values are greater than 200.
* **(<=, 0.8, 50):** At most 80% of the values are less than or equal to 50.
* **(<, 0.01, -5):** Less than 1% of the values are less than -5.
